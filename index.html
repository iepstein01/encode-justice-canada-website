Our Mission

Encode Justice is an international organization fighting for justice in the age of Artificial Intellgience (AI). In Canada, our goal is simple: make AI a political issue.

Our goal is to ensure that AI serves to benefit people, not companies.

  • Advocate for the fair and ethical use of AI by corporations and governments alike

Through these three pillars, we are making AI a political issue by demonstrating how it affects Canadian society and how it can be used to make Canada better.
