About Us

Encode Justice is an international organization fighting for justice in the age of Artificial Intelligence (AI). In Canada, our goal is simple: make AI a political issue.

Growing Importance of AI

AI has rapidly become a part of daily life. From email spam filters to your daily commute, everyone likely encounters some form of AI technology each day. However, while AI can be used in small and beneficial ways, it can also be harmful.


Facial recognition systems, for example, falsely identify people of colour at a higher rate than white people - therefore, when used by police departments, facial recognition amplifies racist policing.
